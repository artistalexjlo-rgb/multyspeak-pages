Some exhibition areas might be closed for events or group bookings. While entry is generally straightforward, some sections may be more engaging for children. Official resale sites for foreigners are virtually non-existent; avoid risky unofficial peer-to-peer transfers where ticket names must match identity documents.

High demand for train and bus tickets, with sales opening approximately one month prior on official websites, means that booking in advance is crucial. Tickets, including standing room options, are often unavailable on third-party platforms like Trip.com. Travelers needing to move between cities during this period should plan for complex itineraries, potential multiple transfers, or consider alternative travel dates. Early hotel bookings in popular tourist areas are also highly recommended.
