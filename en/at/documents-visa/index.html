Cash payments may be suspicious. 4. **Proof of solvency:** Landlords may require proof of your financial stability. A significant security deposit (up to 6 months' rent) might be requested. 5. **Beware of scams:** Be cautious of unusually low prices, requests for advance payment before signing the contract, or unsolicited offers of assistance via private messages. Verify the landlord's legitimacy.
