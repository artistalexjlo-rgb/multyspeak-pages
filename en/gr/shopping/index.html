  • Greece offers a good variety of vegetarian and vegan options: - **Traditional Dishes:** Many traditional dishes are naturally vegan or can be easily adapted. Look for: - **Gemista (or Gemistes):** Stuffed vegetables (tomatoes, peppers) with rice and herbs (ensure no meat is added). - **Spanakorizo:** Spinach and rice. - **Fasolakia:** Green beans cooked in tomato sauce. - **Fasolada:** White bean soup. - **Revithia:** Chickpea soup. - **Horta Vrasta:** Boiled wild greens, often served with lemon and olive oil. - **Dairy & Egg-Based:** Vegetarian options include Greek salads (with feta), various pies (pita), and dishes with eggs. - **Key Ingredients:** Greek cuisine relies heavily on olive oil, lemon, fresh vegetables, and herbs, making many dishes naturally suitable for plant-based diets. - **Sauces:** Tzatziki, a yogurt-based sauce, is common, but vegan alternatives using plant-based yogurt are increasingly available. - **Resources:** Apps like HappyCow can help locate vegan-friendly restaurants.
